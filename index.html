About Me

Born and raised in California, I grew up in San Jose (in Northern California) then moved to Los Angeles (in Southern California). I grew up loving art and nature and started drawing anything I could think of since kindergarten. From dinosaurs to flowers and to cartoon characters. I got into intro to design courses in college and realized I had an interest in creating layouts while learning InDesign. After a few years I changed course and went into Pharmacy as a Pharmacy Technician and stayed that course for over 8 years. I later became a stay-at-home mom after I suffered from an injury back in 2019.

Just at the beginning of 2022 I wanted to make a career change and somehow developed an interest in web development after looking back into graphic design. I realized I loved the idea of learning how to create a webpage and took a few courses on basic coding. I am eager to learn and enthusiastic about becoming great at it. I love and admire all aspects of design and I can't wait to see how far I can go in this journey.
